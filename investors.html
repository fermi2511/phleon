Investor Overview

Phleon is a wearable, AI‑assisted smart sock for early detection of deep vein thrombosis (DVT). Multisensor fusion (IPG, PPG, temperature, motion) with an explainable risk score for home use.

Stage

Pre‑clinical pilot; clinical study design drafted; accepted to accent incubator; AWS PreSeed in preparation.

Market

Large need in post‑operative care, oncology, long‑haul travel, and elderly care; payer‑aligned value via avoided readmissions.

Technology & IP

Cuff‑less sensor fusion (IPG+PPG+motion+temp) with explainable AI, MDR‑oriented dev process; IP strategy underway.

Recent Milestones

  • Prototype smart sock + app mockups
  • Signal acquisition stack finalized (IPG/PPG/temp/motion)
  • Usability feedback from early testers
  • Initial clinical partners engaged

12‑Month Roadmap

  • Pilot expansion and data collection
  • Clinical feasibility study
  • Algorithm validation & explainability
  • MDR pathway preparation (technical file foundations)
