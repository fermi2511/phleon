The future of DVT monitoring starts here.

Our Mission

VenoSense is developing a wearable medical device that enables early and continuous detection of deep vein thrombosis (DVT) risk. By combining biosignal sensors with intelligent feedback, our system helps reduce the risk of silent DVT events.

Technology

Our compression sock integrates multiple sensors: impedance electrodes woven into the fabric, a temperature and PPG sensor in skin contact, and motion sensing. The compact control unit (approx. 50×30×10 mm) is removable for charging and easy washing.
