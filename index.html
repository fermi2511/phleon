Early Detection of Deep Vein Thrombosis — from home

Phleon is a wearable, AI‑assisted smart sock that tracks venous function using bioimpedance (IPG), photoplethysmography (PPG), temperature and motion to estimate real‑time DVT risk.

Investors: Request Deck Join Pilot Program

Currently in pre‑clinical pilot phase.

The Problem

DVT is a frequent, costly, and often silent threat—particularly post‑surgery, during immobility, long travel, or in chronic conditions. Today’s detection is episodic and reactive; continuous, non‑invasive monitoring at home is missing.

Impact

Prevents delayed diagnosis and complications (PE), reduces readmissions and costs.

Users

Post‑op patients, oncology, long‑haul travel, elderly with limited mobility.

Gap

No continuous, user‑friendly DVT risk monitoring for the home setting.

The Solution

A comfortable smart sock with multi‑sensor fusion: IPG (venous impedance), PPG (blood volume), temperature, and motion, combined with an explainable AI risk score and simple user feedback in a mobile app.

  • Continuous
    Home monitoring between visits.
  • Non‑invasive
    Cuff‑less, wearable, comfortable.
  • Actionable
    Real‑time signal and app guidance.
